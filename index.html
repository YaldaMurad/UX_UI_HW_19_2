About Me

I am a dedicated UX/UI designer with a strong educational foundation in both computer science and business administration. My journey into the world of design began at TAFE NSW, where I earned a diploma, setting a solid groundwork for my career. Driven by a passion for creating exceptional user experiences, I further honed my skills through a rigorous UX/UI Bootcamp Certification program at Monash University. This intensive program immersed me in the intricacies of human-centered design principles and equipped me with the tools to craft solutions that prioritize users above all else. My design approach revolves around empathy and a profound understanding of users' needs, aspirations, and pain points. I am committed to crafting seamless, user-centric solutions that not only meet but exceed expectations. With a strong focus on collaboration and problem-solving, I am eager to collaborate with you to bring your vision to life.
